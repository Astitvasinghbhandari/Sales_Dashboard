# Hamleys USA Sales Performance Dashboard
## 1. Business Objective 
This project focuses on analyzing retail sales performance for Hamleys USA across multiple stores and cities.

The primary objective is to evaluate:
Overall business health
Revenue trends over time
Store-level sales performance
Regional contribution to profit
Unit volume vs. value contribution
Drivers of revenue growth

The dashboard is designed to provide leadership with a single, interactive performance view to support decision-making related to expansion, inventory planning, and revenue optimization.

## 2. Business Questions Answered

This dashboard answers key operational and strategic questions:

### Which cities are driving the highest revenue and orders?

### How has revenue evolved month-over-month?

### Which stores are operationally successful vs underperforming?

### Where is customer demand concentrated?

### What time periods generate the highest sales spikes?

### Which product or location characteristics influence revenue growth?

### How do orders and units sold translate into profit?

## 3. Tools & Approach
### Tools Used:

Power BI – Modelling, visualization, dashboard design
DAX – Data cleaning, metric creation, and calculations
Excel / Raw CSV – Original data ingestion

### Data Preparation Process:

Removed null and invalid records
Standardized date formats
Created calculated columns using DAX:
Profit metrics
Revenue aggregations
Order totals
Time intelligence fields (month, week range)
Validated city and store relationships

### Designed measures for:

Profit
Orders
Units sold
Revenue

## 4. Dashboard Components Explained

### KPI Cards (Top Metrics)
Profit: Measures financial health
Orders: Tracks volume of transactions
Units Sold: Indicates demand level
These provide quick executive summary insights.

### Total Orders by Store (Bar Chart)
This compares operational performance across cities such as:
Mexico City
Guadalajara
Monterrey
It highlights:
Top-performing stores
Underutilized locations

### Revenue Trend by Month (Horizontal Bars)
Shows:
Seasonal demand patterns
Revenue cycles
Growth and slowdown periods
This helps in forecasting and campaign planning.

### Slicers (Interactivity)
Filters available:
Store location
Date range
This enables targeted investigation into:
A specific city
A specific time window
Store-wise performance diagnostics

### Key Influencer Visual

Analyzes factors that directly influence revenue changes.
It identifies:
Which variables increase or reduce revenue
Which product segments and locations have the strongest impact

## 5. Business Interpretation
### Overall Position:

The company shows strong sales consistency
Major revenue is concentrated in a few high-performing cities
Not all stores contribute equally — optimization is needed

### Opportunities Identified:
High-performing stores should be replicated
Weak locations require targeted promotions or optimization
Peak months indicate strong seasonality — inventory must align with demand
Revenue drivers identified using influencer analysis can be leveraged for growth
